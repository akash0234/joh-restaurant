A Day Out to Joh’s Restaurant: From a Foodie’s Perspective

02/07/2020

If you are a foodie, and love to experiment with food, you must have had explored multiple restaurants. In Australia, there is a myriad of restaurants, and exploring all of them for a single person is almost impossible. To taste the most delicacies in Australia, you can shortlist a few restaurants and explore the taste of every dish.

When it comes to choosing the top restaurants in Australia, Joh's Restaurant definitely comes on the list. If you want to experience true Mediterranean hospitality, don’t forget to visit Joh’s Restaurant. It is the place to have chitchat over a coffee. Every time you order, you get nourishing food with exceptional taste on your plate. If you want to explore the tastiest food here, don’t forget to taste the following –

1. Coffee:

Nothing can make you more refreshed than a cup of coffee, especially when it is professionally made. Many things can happen over a cup of coffee. So, order a cappuccino or expresso and let the chitchat begins.
Taste – 5/5
Portion – 5/5

2. Beef burger:

Choose a companion with your coffee wisely. The widest dishes of snacks in Joh's restaurant can sometimes confuse you with what to order. If you are a non-vegetarian, I would suggest you taste the Aussie Beef burger here. The finger-licking taste will insist you order another one. The juicy minced beef, beetroot, and tomato will melt in your mouth with every bite.
Taste – 5/5
Portion - 4.5/5

3. Greek loaded fries:

If you want to experience something different in veg with your coffee, order a plate of frozen fries piled with a moreish Greek-inspired topping of olives, feta, cucumber and tomatoes. Just one plate is enough to share between the two.
Taste - 5/5
Portion – 5/5

4. Falafel:

Taste the best falafel ever only at Joh's Restaurant. The crispy falafel perfectly goes with a cup of coffee. Falafel is carefully prepared with broad beans, garlic, chickpeas, baby onion and mixed spice. It can be both veg or non-veg.
Taste 5/5
Portion – 4.5/5

If you want to experience the delicious delicacies and feel the true Mediterranean hospitality, visit Joh's restaurant today! Visit the restaurant or order food online on Fridays to get up to 49% off on your orders.
